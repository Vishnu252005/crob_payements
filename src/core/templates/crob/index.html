What is CROB?

CROB ( Centre for Robotics TKMCE) is a student-driven community focused on robotics and multidisciplinary innovation. We aim to bring together students passionate about robotics, providing them with the tools and support they need to explore, learn, and create groundbreaking technologies.

Vision

To be a leading community that fosters innovation in robotics and related fields, where students collaborate, learn, and solve real-world challenges through cutting-edge technology.

Mission

Our mission is to create a platform that enables students to explore robotics through hands-on projects, interdisciplinary collaboration, and mentorship, helping them develop the skills needed for the future of technology.

Why CROB?

Creating a community of like-minded students interested in the field of robotics

Objectives of CROB

  • Year-wise collaboration projects
  • Forming a multi-disciplinary domain
  • Center is run by students
  • Access to lab facilities
  • Beginner to advanced level projects
  • Monthly community meet-ups
  • Participation in the eYantra competition
  • Project mentoring
  • Serving as a research center

Frequently Asked Questions

What is CROB, and how is it different from a traditional club?

CROB is a vibrant community focused on collaborative growth, knowledge sharing, and fostering meaningful connections among its members. Unlike traditional clubs, which often have limited scope and structure, CROB encourages open participation and inclusivity, allowing members to contribute, learn, and grow together through events, discussions, and shared projects.

Who can join the CROB community?

Anyone who shares an interest in our community’s values and goals is welcome to join CROB. We believe in inclusivity, so students, professionals, hobbyists, and anyone who is passionate about contributing and learning from a dynamic community can be a part of it.

What kind of activities does CROB organize?

CROB organizes a wide range of activities, including collaborative workshops, webinars, skill-sharing sessions, networking events, and community projects. Our activities are focused on bringing members together to learn, collaborate, and work on real-world challenges while building valuable skills.

Is there a membership fee to join CROB?

CROB offers flexible membership options to cater to different needs. While some events and resources are freely accessible to all members, we also offer premium membership tiers with additional perks, such as access to exclusive events, mentoring sessions, and premium content.

How can I contribute to CROB as a member?

CROB thrives on active participation from its members. You can contribute by sharing your skills and knowledge during our events, participating in group projects, mentoring fellow members, and even suggesting or leading new initiatives. We encourage members to take ownership and drive the community forward.

What are the benefits of joining CROB?

As a CROB member, you’ll gain access to a supportive network of individuals with diverse skills and experiences. You’ll be able to collaborate on exciting projects, attend events and workshops, build your personal and professional skills, and become part of a community dedicated to continuous learning and development.
